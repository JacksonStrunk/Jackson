Monarch Caterpillar

Butterfly Stage

Chrysalis Stage

After hatching from a cluster of eggs a caterpillar will eat milkweed plants. It will continue to only eat for about 2 weeks to keep growing. Once its done eating it will find a place to attach itself then it will start the first metamorphosis, the chrysalis stage.
